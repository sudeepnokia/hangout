Quantum Computing Fundamentals

A curated learning path to understand the intersection of quantum mechanics and VLSI design.

1. Fundamental Concepts

Start here. A high-level overview of the core principles of quantum computing, including qubits, superposition, and entanglement.

2. Quantum Concepts: A Deep Dive

Explore the mathematical and conceptual foundations of quantum computation, including the Bloch Sphere and quantum gates.

3. Qubit Fabrication

Learn how qubits are built, and how we can leverage semiconductor manufacturing processes to create them at scale.

4. Cryogenic CMOS for Quantum Control

Understand the challenges and solutions for building the electronics needed to control qubits at ultra-low temperatures.

5. Photonic Interconnects for Scalability

Discover how light can be used to connect modular quantum processors, enabling larger and more powerful systems.
