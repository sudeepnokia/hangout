Your Italy & Greece Adventure

A 9-Day Itinerary from Rome to Athens

Pre-Trip Preparation Checklist

3 Months Before (Late June) - Apply for Schengen Visa

As Indian passport holders, you need a Schengen visa. Since your first port of entry is Italy, you'll apply through them.
Action: Book an appointment at VFS Global in Bangalore.
Timeline: Processing takes 15-45 days,It's highly recommended to apply at least 2-3 months in advance to avoid any last-minute stress. so start early! You'll need flight bookings, hotel reservations, and travel insurance for your application.

1 Week Before - Pack & Final Prep

Things to Pack:

  • Clothing: Light layers are key.Weather in late Sep/early Oct is pleasant but can be unpredictable. Expect days around 18-24°C and cooler evenings. Pack t-shirts, a light jacket/cardigan, a warmer sweater, and comfortable trousers/jeans. Include a scarf for visiting religious sites.
  • Footwear: Very comfortable walking shoes are essential.
  • Essentials: Passport, visa, travel insurance, universal travel adapter, reusable water bottle, portable power bank.

Connectivity (SIM Card):

  • Local SIM: Upon arrival in Rome, you can buy a tourist SIM from providers like TIM or Vodafone (~€25-€30 for a month with ample data).
  • eSIM: For convenience, consider an eSIM.Activate it upon landing without needing a physical SIM. Check providers like Airalo or Holafly before you travel. Purchase and install it before you leave India.
