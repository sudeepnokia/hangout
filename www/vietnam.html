Pre-Trip Preparation Checklist

1-2 Months Before (July-August) - Get Your Visa

As Indian passport holders, you are required to have a visa to enter Vietnam.
Action: Apply for an e-Visa online via the official portal.The official Vietnam Immigration Department e-Visa website is evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn. The process is straightforward and takes 3-5 working days.
Important: Apply at least a few weeks in advance. The e-Visa costs $25 USD for a single-entry, 90-day tourist visa. Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from your date of entry.

1 Week Before - Pack & Final Prep

Things to Pack:

  • Clothing: Lightweight and breathable clothes.North Vietnam in Oct will be pleasant (22-28°C) but can have rain. Pack t-shirts, shorts, a light rain jacket, and one warmer layer for evenings or the overnight cruise. Modest clothing (covering shoulders/knees) is needed for temples.
  • Footwear: Comfortable walking shoes/sandals are a must.
  • Essentials: Passport, e-Visa printout, insurance, universal adapter, power bank, Vietnamese Dong (VND) cash.

Connectivity (SIM Card):

  • Local SIM: Buy at Hanoi's Noi Bai (HAN) airport. Viettel has the best coverage, especially in rural areas like Ninh Binh & Ha Long Bay. A tourist pack costs ~150k-300k VND ($6-12 USD).
  • eSIM: Consider an eSIM for convenience.Activate it upon landing without a physical SIM. Check providers like Airalo or Holafly before you travel.
