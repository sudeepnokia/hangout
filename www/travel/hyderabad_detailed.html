Hyderabad Trip Itinerary

15-17 Aug 2025 – 7 travellers (two couples & family of three)

This itinerary suggests a relaxed but comprehensive trip to Hyderabad. Arrival and departure are at Secunderabad Junction. Distances are manageable, but traffic can be unpredictable. The Hyderabad Metro is a convenient way to explore, running from 06:30 to 22:30. For group travel, a rented 7-seater (e.g., Toyota Innova) is recommended.

07:00 - Arrive & Check-in

Arrive at Secunderabad Junction. Walk or take a short auto to your hotel. If rooms aren’t ready, leave baggage at reception.

Transport: Walk/auto (5-10 min)

09:30 - Birla Science Museum & Planetarium

Interactive science exhibits, archaeology, and space museum. Open 10:30 AM - 8:00 PM. Entry: ₹200 (museum), ₹300 (museum + planetarium).

Transport: Metro from Secunderabad East to Lakdi Ka Pul, then auto/cab.

14:00 - Tank Bund & Hussain Sagar Lake

Promenade with 33 bronze statues and views of the Buddha statue. Open 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM. Boating optional.

Transport: Auto/cab from Birla centre (~2 km).

16:00 - Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge

Modern bridge with walking paths, illuminated in the evening. Open 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM.

Transport: Metro to Hitec City station, then auto (2 km), or direct cab.

08:00 - Ramoji Film City

Full-day tour of a massive film-themed park with sets, shows, and rides. Open 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM. Tickets: ₹1,450 (adult), ₹1,250 (child).

Transport: 7-seater cab (~1 hr each way). Leave by 8 AM.

18:30 - Dinner at Hotel Shadab

Widely regarded for its biryani and kebabs. Located in the old city near Charminar.

Transport: Cab en route from Ramoji Film City.

08:30 - Charminar

Hyderabad’s iconic monument with a mosque and viewing gallery. Open 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM. Entry: ₹25 (Indian), ₹300 (foreigner).

Transport: Cab/auto to the old city (~7 km).

11:00 - Chowmahalla Palace

Former seat of the Asaf Jahi dynasty with grand halls and vintage cars. Open 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (closed Fridays). Entry: ₹100 (adult), ₹40 (child).

Transport: Walk/auto from Charminar (~700 m).

14:00 - Salar Jung Museum

One of the world's largest private art collections. Open 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (closed Fridays). Entry: ₹50 (Indian), ₹500 (foreigner).

Transport: Auto/cab from Chowmahalla Palace (~1.5 km).

16:00 - Depart

Collect luggage and head to Secunderabad Junction for your 17:25 train.

Transport: Cab/auto (30-45 min from old city).

  • Royalton Hotel, Abids: Central location, spacious rooms. Approx. ₹3,500-4,500/night.
  • Hotel Sai Krupa, Chikkadpally: Budget-friendly option with good amenities. Approx. ₹2,500-3,500/night.
  • Hotel Royal Reve, Secunderabad: 3-star hotel near the station. Approx. ₹3,500-4,500/night.

  • Hotel Shadab: Famous for Hyderabadi biryani and haleem.
  • RTC X Road/Ashok Nagar: Vibrant street food scene.
  • Kamat Hotel: Popular for vegetarian South Indian meals.

  • Weather: Expect occasional showers in mid-August. Carry umbrellas and raincoats.
  • Dress Code: Modest attire is recommended for historical and religious sites.
  • Transport: A 7-seater cab is convenient for group travel. Autos are good for short journeys.
  • Entry Fees: Carry cash for tickets. Some places offer discounts for cashless payments.
  • Safety: Be mindful of personal belongings in crowded areas.
