Phase 1: The Binomial Expansion

The binomial theorem gives the expansion of (1+x)ⁿ:

(1+x)ⁿ = ⁿC₀ + ⁿC₁x + ⁿC₂x² + ... + ⁿCₙxⁿ

This is a polynomial in x, and the coefficients are the familiar binomial coefficients.
