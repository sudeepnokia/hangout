Journey to the Land of the Thunder Dragon

A 9-Day Himalayan Adventure in Bhutan

Pre-Trip Preparation Checklist

1-2 Months Before - Permits & SDF

As Indian passport holders, you don't need a visa, but an entry permit is mandatory.
Action: You can get the permit at the Paro Airport arrival hall or at the Immigration Office in Phuentsholing if entering by road. You'll need your passport or Voter ID card.
Sustainable Development Fee (SDF): Indian tourists must pay an SDF of ₹1,200 per person per night.This fee goes towards funding social welfare, developmental activities, and environmental conservation in Bhutan. It must be paid in advance.
Important: Book your hotels and have a confirmed itinerary before applying for the permit online or on arrival.

2 Weeks Before - Pack & Final Prep

Things to Pack:

  • Clothing: Layers are essential.Autumn in Bhutan (Sep-Oct) has pleasant days (15-20°C) but cold nights (5-10°C). Pack warm jackets, thermals, sweaters, and comfortable trousers. Modest attire is required for visiting monasteries (Dzongs).
  • Footwear: Sturdy, comfortable hiking/walking shoes are a must, especially for the Tiger's Nest trek.
  • Essentials: Passport/Voter ID, permit confirmation, power bank, universal adapter (Types D, F, G), and sufficient Indian Rupees (INR), which is widely accepted.

Connectivity (SIM Card):

  • Local SIM: Easily available at Paro airport. Bhutan Telecom and TashiCell are the main providers. A tourist SIM with data costs around ₹700-1000.
  • Indian SIMs: May work on international roaming, but can be very expensive. A local SIM is recommended.

Weather Prediction (Late Sep - Early Oct)

This is one of the best times to visit Bhutan. The monsoon has just ended, leaving the landscape lush and green.
Paro & Thimphu: Expect clear blue skies and pleasant daytime temperatures of 15-20°C. Nights will be chilly, dropping to 5-10°C.
Punakha: Being at a lower altitude, it will be warmer, with daytime temperatures around 20-25°C.
Rainfall: Minimal chance of rain, perfect for trekking and sightseeing.
